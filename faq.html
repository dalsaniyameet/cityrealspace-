Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about buying, selling and renting properties.

Home FAQ
How do I start the property buying process with City Real Space?
Simply browse our listings, shortlist properties you like, and click "Inquiry" or "Chat on WhatsApp". Our property advisor will contact you within 30 minutes to understand your requirements and schedule a free site visit.
Are all properties on City Real Space verified?
Yes. Every property listed on our platform is physically verified by our expert team. We check RERA registration, ownership documents and site conditions before listing. You can trust that what you see is what you get.
Is the site visit free of charge?
Absolutely! Site visits are completely free. Our agent will accompany you to the property, answer all your questions and help you make an informed decision — with zero pressure.
How do I list my property on City Real Space?
Click on "Sell → List Property" in the navigation menu, fill in your property details, upload photos and submit. Our team will review and publish your listing within 24 hours. You can also WhatsApp us directly at +91 93775 31247.
What is the brokerage fee for selling a property?
Our brokerage is competitive and transparent — typically 1% to 2% of the transaction value. For select projects, we offer zero brokerage deals. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your property type and location.
Can I find rental properties on City Real Space?
Yes! We have a wide range of residential and commercial rental properties across Ahmedabad. Use the "Buy → Rent" filter to browse available rentals. You can filter by area, budget and property type.
What documents are needed for renting a property?
Typically you need: Aadhaar card, PAN card, last 3 months' salary slips or bank statements, and passport-size photos. For commercial rentals, GST registration and company documents may also be required.
Does City Real Space help with home loans?
Yes! We have tie-ups with leading banks and NBFCs including SBI, HDFC, ICICI and Axis Bank. Our home loan experts will help you compare rates, check eligibility and process your application — completely free of charge.
What is the maximum home loan I can get?
Most banks offer up to 80–90% of the property value as a home loan. The exact amount depends on your income, credit score, age and existing liabilities. Our loan advisors can give you a free eligibility assessment within minutes.
What is RERA and why is it important?
RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) is a government body that regulates the real estate sector. A RERA-registered project ensures the builder is accountable, the project is legally approved and your investment is protected. Always buy RERA-registered properties.
What documents should I check before buying a property?
Key documents to verify: Title deed, RERA registration certificate, approved building plan, NOC from relevant authorities, encumbrance certificate, and sale agreement. Our legal team can help you verify all documents before you commit.
