Information We Collect

City Real Space collects information you provide directly to us when you register, submit an inquiry, or use our services.

  • Name, email address and mobile number
  • Property preferences and search history
  • Location data when you use our map features
  • Device information and browser type
  • Communication records with our agents

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain and improve our services.

  • To connect you with relevant property listings and agents
  • To send you property alerts and market updates (with your consent)
  • To process inquiries and schedule site visits
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With property agents and builders to fulfill your inquiry requests
  • With trusted service providers who assist in our operations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience on our platform.

  • Essential cookies for site functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand usage patterns
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences at any time.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. This includes SSL encryption, secure servers and regular security audits.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications
  • Right to data portability
